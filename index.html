Meet Vy

With over 10 years of experience and a deep-seated passion for the artistry of nails and lashes, Vy has dedicated her career to the pursuit of perfection. Her meticulous eye for detail and commitment to using only the finest techniques have earned her a loyal following of clients who followed her to her beautiful new location in Crystal Lake.

For Vy, it's about more than just a service—it's about the connection. She believes every client deserves a moment of tranquility and a look that makes them feel effortlessly beautiful. Whether it's a subtle enhancement or a bold transformation, Vy's mission is to ensure you leave her chair feeling like the most confident version of yourself.
